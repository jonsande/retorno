MODULE DOSSIER — bus_stabilizer

Role
- Phase-control and noise-damping module for aging distribution buses.

Operational effect
- Dampens transient spikes and line noise.
- Improves load-sharing smoothness and grid quality resilience.

Integration notes
- Installation may require brief bus resynchronization.
- Side effect: slightly higher idle draw.

Field note
- Deck crews nicknamed these units "calmers".
