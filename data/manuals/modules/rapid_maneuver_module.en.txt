MODULE DOSSIER — rapid_maneuver_module

Role
- Mobility core for high-response drone builds.

Operational effect
- Scope: `drone` (slot cost: 1).
- Reduces move ETA (`move_time_mult`).
- Reduces deploy ETA (`deploy_time_mult`), including emergency deploy job timing.
- Reduces survey ETA (`survey_time_mult`).
- Tradeoff: lowers effective maximum integrity (`integrity_max_add` negative).

Integration notes
- Installed/uninstalled through bay operation with nominal bay requirements.
- Effective integrity cap impacts:
  - deploy/recall safety margins,
  - passive/active repair targets,
  - drone status interpretation.
- Pair with `reinforced_frame` when mobility is required but survivability floor is too low.

Field note
- Chosen when travel cadence and sensor/survey tempo are more valuable than structural margin.
