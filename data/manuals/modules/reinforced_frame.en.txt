MODULE DOSSIER — reinforced_frame

Role
- Structural reinforcement package for survivability-biased drone builds.

Operational effect
- Scope: `drone` (slot cost: 1).
- Increases effective maximum integrity (`integrity_max_add`).
- Raises damage tolerance and effective repair ceiling.
- Tradeoff: slightly increases movement time (`move_time_mult`).

Integration notes
- Passive bay repair and active repair jobs target effective maxima and can recover up to the reinforced cap.
- Installed/uninstalled via bay operation (`drone_bay=NOMINAL`, distribution online, target drone docked).
- Commonly paired with repair or mobility modules to offset integrity-side tradeoffs.

Field note
- Recommended in high-radiation or high-risk salvage loops where attrition is sustained.
