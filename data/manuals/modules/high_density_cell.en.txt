MODULE DOSSIER — high_density_cell

Role
- Endurance battery package for extended drone operations.

Operational effect
- Scope: `drone` (slot cost: 1).
- Increases effective maximum battery (`battery_max_add`).
- Extends mission endurance before low-battery gating and autorecall.
- Tradeoff: slightly increases movement time (`move_time_mult`).

Integration notes
- Bay charging respects effective maxima and will charge up to the new battery cap.
- Installed/uninstalled via bay operation (`drone_bay=NOMINAL`, distribution online, target drone docked).
- Works well with cargo or repair-biased presets where cycle duration is long.

Field note
- Typical pick when the bottleneck is return frequency, not action speed.
