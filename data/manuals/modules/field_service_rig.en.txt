MODULE DOSSIER — field_service_rig

Role
- Field maintenance rig for repair-focused drone builds.

Operational effect
- Scope: `drone` (slot cost: 1).
- Reduces active repair ETA (`repair_time_mult`).
- Reduces effective repair fail probability (`repair_fail_p_mult`) using the existing repair failure model.
- Reduces effective repair scrap cost (`repair_scrap_cost_mult`) for both successful and failed repair attempts.
- Tradeoff: increases movement time (`move_time_mult`).

Integration notes
- Installed/uninstalled via bay operation commands:
  - `drone install <drone_id> field_service_rig`
  - `drone uninstall <drone_id> field_service_rig`
- Bay operation requirements:
  - target drone docked in `drone_bay`,
  - `drone_bay == NOMINAL`,
  - `energy_distribution != OFFLINE`.
- Stacks multiplicatively with other drone modules under profile clamps.

Field note
- Preferred in long-duration recovery loops where failed repairs are expensive.
