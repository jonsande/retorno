MODULE DOSSIER — utility_cargo_frame

Role
- Utility frame extension for cargo-biased drone builds.

Operational effect
- Scope: `drone` (slot cost: 1).
- Increases effective drone cargo capacity (`cargo_capacity_add`).
- Directly improves `drone salvage scrap` throughput by reducing ETA per requested amount.
- Tradeoff: increases movement time (`move_time_mult`).

Integration notes
- Installed/uninstalled as a bay operation (`drone install` / `drone uninstall`).
- Subject to bay operation requirements (`drone_bay=NOMINAL`, distribution online, drone docked).
- Designed for salvage-oriented presets; combine with endurance or mobility modules depending on route profile.

Field note
- Best return when salvage windows are short and scrap extraction dominates mission time.
