status

Purpose
- Show consolidated mission-critical snapshot: power, systems, transit posture, and alert context.

Operational behavior
- Designed as first-look command after wake, incident, or major action.

Usage notes
- Use as entry point, then drill down with `alerts`, `diag`, `jobs`, and domain commands.

Related commands
- `alerts`, `power status`, `ship systems`, `drone status [drone_id]`
