ship sectors
nav map sectors

Purpose
- Inspect known sectors from ship-internal and world-navigation perspectives.

Operational behavior
- `ship sectors`: lists internal ship sectors and subsystem placement context.
- `nav map sectors`: lists known world sectors and navigation coverage.

RETORNO internal-sector set
- `DCK-A1`, `STS-BAY`, `LFS-01`, `PWR-A1`, `PWR-A2`, `PRP-R1`, `BRG-01`, `SNS-R1`, `DRN-BAY`, `CRG-01`

Sector-id convention
- Internal sector ids are canonical/reusable per ship type/functional position.
- Resolve uniqueness using ship/node context (for example `RETORNO_SHIP:BRG-01`).

Related commands
- `locate <id>`, `nav map graph`, `status`
