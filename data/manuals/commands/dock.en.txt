dock <node_id>

Purpose
- Dock RETORNO at a known node and transition from orbit/transit posture to docked posture.

Requirements (gating)
- Ship must not be in transit.
- Current position must already be the target node (`<node_id>`).
- Target must be a known contact and dock-capable node.

Behavior
- Dock queues a timed job.
- Dock can be interrupted if context changes before completion.
- `job cancel <job_id>` can abort an active dock job.

Common block reasons
- `in_transit`
- `not_at_node`
- target not known / node missing / docking not allowed

Related commands
- `undock`, `jobs`, `job cancel <job_id>`, `status`
