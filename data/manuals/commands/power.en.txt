power status
power plan cruise|normal
power on <system_id>
power off <system_id>

Purpose
- Inspect and control electrical posture of RETORNO.

Operational model
- `power status`: shows SoC, `P_gen`, `P_load`, `Q`, brownout, and margins.
- `power plan cruise|normal`: applies profile-level load policy.
- `power on/off <system_id>`: manual power shedding/restoration for a subsystem.

Requirements and limits
- `power on <system_id>` requires recoverable system health and dependency chain satisfaction.
- In unstable conditions, automatic policy may override subsystem states to preserve survivability.
- In critical power state, only essential control actions are allowed.

Operator guidance
- Stabilize `Q` before complex operations.
- Prefer shedding non-critical loads before touching critical systems.

Related commands
- `status`, `diag <system_id>`, `alerts`, `alerts explain <alert_key>`
