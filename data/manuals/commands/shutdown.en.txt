shutdown <service_name>

Purpose
- Stop a running service daemon for load reduction, containment, or recovery sequencing.

Operational behavior
- Stops service if running and updates subsystem service state.
- May reduce load but can disable dependent workflows.

Usage notes
- Prefer targeted shutdowns; avoid stopping critical services unless required for survival.

Related commands
- `boot <service_name>`, `power status`, `diag <system_id>`
