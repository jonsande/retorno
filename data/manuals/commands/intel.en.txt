intel
- Lists known navigation intel (latest first).
Usage: intel | intel <amount> | intel all
- Columns:
  ID: intel item identifier.
  kind: intel type (node/link/sector/coord).
  what: short description of the target.
  conf: confidence (0.00â€“1.00).
  source: origin of the intel (may include @ref).
  age: time since recorded.
- Intel kinds:
  node: a specific known node/contact (e.g., HARBOR_12).
  sector: a known sector identifier (e.g., S+0001_-0002_+0000).
  link: a known route between two nodes (A -> B).
  coord: exact coordinates (x,y,z) that point to a nav point.

intel show <intel_id>
- Shows details and provenance for a specific intel item.

intel import <path>
- Imports navigation intel from a file.
- Supported markers: NODE:, LINK:, SECTOR:, COORD:
- Optional blocks:
  [INTEL] ... [/INTEL]
  - If the block contains a LINK, it is imported as a route.
  - If it contains a node_id, name, or sector id, the matching authored node is added as a known contact.
  - Corrupt intel may fail to resolve or yield a new (unknown) hub contact.
- Files under /data/nav, /mail or /logs may auto-import on cat.

intel export <path>
- Exports current intel to a file under /logs/nav or /data/nav.
