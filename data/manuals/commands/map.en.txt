- `map` defaults to `map graph`.
- `map ship` shows RETORNO ship sectors (same content as `sectors`).
- `map graph` shows the known global route graph (which nodes link to which, and which do not).
- `map graph <node_id>` focuses the graph view on one known node.
- `map path <node_id>` shows the shortest known path from your current node, if any.

Dependencies
- `map ship`: no extra dependency.
- `map graph` and `map path`: require `data_core` >= `limited` and `datad` running.

Notes
- `no_link` means "no known direct link yet", not "impossible forever".
- If no path exists, use `uplink`, `scan`, and `route solve` to discover bridge links.
