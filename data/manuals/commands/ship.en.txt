ship survey

Purpose
- Provide ship-level operational snapshots and survey workflows.

Operational behavior
- `ship status`: condensed ship health/power overview.
- `ship systems`: subsystem table with state/health/service context.
- `ship sectors`: internal topology and placement.
- `ship survey`: structured survey job/event pipeline (requires sensor/data stack availability).

RETORNO canonical internal sectors
- `DCK-A1`: Dock / Airlock
- `STS-BAY`: Stasis Bay
- `LFS-01`: Life Support
- `PWR-A1`: Power Core
- `PWR-A2`: Power Distribution
- `PRP-R1`: Propulsion Room (reserved)
- `BRG-01`: Bridge / Command
- `SNS-R1`: Sensors Room
- `DRN-BAY`: Drone Bay
- `CRG-01`: Cargo Hold

Sector-id convention
- Sector ids are canonical and reusable across ships.
- Global uniqueness comes from context (`ship_id:sector_id`), not from bare sector id.

Usage notes
- Use `ship systems` + `alerts` to triage failures before long operations.

Related commands
- `status`, `diag <system_id>`, `power status`, `jobs`
