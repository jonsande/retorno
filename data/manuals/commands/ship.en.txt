Purpose
- Provide ship-level operational snapshots and survey workflows.

Operational behavior
- `ship status`: condensed ship health/power overview.
- `ship systems`: subsystem table with state/health/service context.
- `ship sectors`: internal topology and placement.
- `ship survey`: structured survey job/event pipeline (requires sensor/data stack availability).

Usage notes
- Use `ship systems` + `alerts` to triage failures before long operations.

Related commands
- `status`, `diag <system_id>`, `power status`, `jobs`
