ship sectors
ship survey <target>

Description
- `ship sectors`: show RETORNO internal sectors and their tags.
- `ship survey <target>`: concise telemetry for one target.

Valid `ship survey` target
- Any world `node_id`.
- `RETORNO_SHIP`.
- Active `state.ship.ship_id`.

Accepted aliases (hidden in `help`)
- `ship map` -> `ship sectors`

Dependencies
- `ship sectors`: depends on `core_os`.
- `ship survey <target>` depends on:
  - `core_os`
  - `sensors >= limited`
  - `sensord` running
  - `data_core >= limited`
  - `datad` running

`ship survey` v0 output
- `id`, `name`, `kind`.
- `sector` and `coords`.
- `distance`.
- Basic availability/intel flags (`visited`, `known_contact`, `known_route_from_current`, `in_sensor_range`).
