inventory
inventory audit

Purpose
- Read current cargo manifest snapshot and reconcile it when stale.

Operational behavior
- `inventory` shows last synchronized scrap/modules data.
- `inventory audit` queues manifest reconciliation job.
- `cargo` is an equivalent alias family.

Usage notes
- Run audit after salvage/install activity if counts seem inconsistent.

Related commands
- `cargo`, `inventory update`, `modules`, `jobs`
