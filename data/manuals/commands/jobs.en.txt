jobs
jobs <amount>
jobs all

Purpose
- Inspect queued/running jobs and recent completions/failures.

Operational behavior
- Default view shows active jobs and bounded recent history.
- `jobs <amount>` limits completed/failed entries.
- `jobs all` prints full retained history.

Usage notes
- Use job id with `job cancel <job_id>` to abort active tasks.

Related commands
- `job cancel <job_id>`, `status`, `logs`
