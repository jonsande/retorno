drone repair <drone_id> <target_id>
repair <system_id> --selftest

Purpose
- Restore ship-system health or drone integrity through active repair workflows.

Repair modes
- `drone repair <drone_id> <target_id>`: field repair by deployed operator drone.
- `repair <system_id> --selftest`: internal self-test repair cycle (requires `selftest_rig`).

Requirements (gating)
- Drone repair requires deployed operator drone, minimum battery, and valid target.
- Drone-to-drone repair requires exact co-location between operator and target drone.
- Both modes require enough scrap for initial reservation.

Job outcomes
- Repair jobs have a low chance of failure.
- On failure, only part of the reserved scrap is consumed; the remainder is refunded.

Operational notes
- Drone repair consumes operator battery and reserved scrap.
- Self-test repair is slower and more scrap-efficient per health unit.
- In critical power state, non-critical repairs are allowed but warned as high-risk tradeoff.

Related commands
- `diag <system_id>`, `drone status [drone_id]`, `jobs`, `alerts`
