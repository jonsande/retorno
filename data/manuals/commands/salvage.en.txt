drone salvage scrap <drone_id> <node_id> <amount>
drone salvage module <drone_id> [node_id]
drone salvage drone <drone_id> <node_id>
drone salvage drones <drone_id> <node_id>
drone salvage data <drone_id> <node_id>

Purpose
- Run drone recovery operations for materials, modules, recoverable drones, and data signatures.

Requirements (gating)
- Operator drone must be deployed, valid, and with minimum battery for task execution.
- Target node must be valid and context-compatible with drone location/ship docking state.
- Action-specific salvage availability must exist (scrap/module/drone/data).

Behavior
- Salvage actions queue timed jobs (duration depends on action and payload).
- Successful salvage updates cargo/intel/world depletion state.
- Salvage operations consume drone battery and carry operational risk from environment/degradation.
- `drone salvage data` reports explicit counters: `new`, `already_mounted`, `total`.
- `new` are files mounted in this operation; `already_mounted` were already present at `/remote/<node_id>/`.

Common block reasons
- drone not deployed / low battery / missing target
- ship not docked at drone world-node location when required
- no eligible salvage left for requested action

Related commands
- `drone survey <drone_id> <node_id>`, `inventory`, `modules`, `intel`, `jobs`
