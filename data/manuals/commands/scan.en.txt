scan

Purpose
- Perform local signal scan and update nearby contact intelligence.

Requirements (gating)
- Sensor stack must be available for scan workflow (`sensors` state and `sensord` service).
- Power state must allow active scanning operations.

Operational behavior
- Updates local contact awareness and may reveal scan events.

Related commands
- `boot sensord`, `diag sensors`, `nav map contacts`, `route solve <node_id>`
