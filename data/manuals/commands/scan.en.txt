scan

Purpose
- Perform local signal scan and update nearby contact intelligence.

Requirements (gating)
- Sensor stack must be available for scan workflow (`sensors` state and `sensord` service).
- Power state must allow active scanning operations.

Operational behavior
- Queues a scan job with ETA based on `Balance.SCAN_TIME_S`.
- On completion, updates local contact awareness and may reveal detection events.
- If new contacts are discovered, it emits `signal_detected` and its mapped cue.
- If operating context changes before completion, the job may be interrupted.

Related commands
- `boot sensord`, `diag sensors`, `jobs`, `nav map contacts`, `route solve <node_id>`
