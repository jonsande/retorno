hibernate until_arrival
hibernate <years>

Purpose
- Advance long-duration transit time with reduced output noise.

Requirements (gating)
- Ship must be in transit.
- Blocked when `life_support` is degraded enough to forbid hibernation.
- Blocked in critical power quality / brownout conditions.

Operational behavior
- `until_arrival`: sleeps until current transit completes.
- `<years>`: sleeps target duration in large chunks.
- Before it becomes effective, hibernation plays a diegetic cryogenization sequence and a visible 10-second countdown.
- When the countdown reaches 0, the log window is cleared and cryostasis takes effect.
- Wakes on critical events that require operator attention.
- While in transit, hibernation also wakes when ambient radiation crosses the configured threshold (`HIBERNATE_WAKE_ENV_RAD_THRESHOLD_RAD_PER_S`).

Related commands
- `nav <node_id>`, `nav abort`, `status`, `alerts`
