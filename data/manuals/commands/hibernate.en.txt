hibernate until_arrival | hibernate <years>
- Advances time in large chunks during transit.
- until_arrival sleeps until the current trip ends.
- Suppresses intermediate spam; shows arrival and critical events.
- Blocked if life_support is degraded, power quality is critical (Q < 0.40), or brownout is active.
- At low SoC (below 0.25) a warning is issued before proceeding.
