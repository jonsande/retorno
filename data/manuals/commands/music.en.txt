music list
music play <track_id>
music stop
music volume <0-100>
music status

Purpose
- Inspect and control diegetic music playback from the ship music archive.

Operational behavior
- `music list` scans the onboard music archive and lists each available track id.
- `music play <track_id>` plays one track on the music channel without interrupting ambient or alert audio.
- `music stop` stops the current music track.
- `music volume <0-100>` sets the current music playback volume for the session and saved operator profile.
- `music status` shows whether music is currently playing, which track is active, the configured volume, and whether ambient ducking is active.

Usage notes
- Tracks are discovered from `retorno/data/music`.
- Music does not autoplay the next track when the current one ends.
- While music is active, ambient audio is slightly attenuated according to `audio_config.json`.
- If global audio is disabled with `config set audio off`, music playback is suppressed.

Related commands
- `config show`, `config set audio <on|off>`, `config set ambientsound <on|off>`
