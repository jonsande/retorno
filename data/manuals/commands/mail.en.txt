mail inbox
mail read <id|latest>

Purpose
- Access mission mailbox artifacts and message payloads.

Operational behavior
- `mail inbox` lists available message ids/subjects.
- `mail read <id>` opens one message.
- `mail read latest` opens most recent inbox message.
- Localized variant is preferred when available.

Related commands
- `ls /mail/inbox`, `cat <path>`, `logs`
