boot <service_name>

Purpose
- Boot a subsystem service daemon (for example `sensord`, `datad`, `securityd`).

Requirements (gating)
- Service must exist and belong to an installed ship subsystem.
- Target subsystem must be recoverable (not destroyed) and with dependencies satisfied.
- Power state must allow boot:
  - non-critical boots are blocked in critical power state (including active brownout),
  - non-critical boots are blocked when power quality is critical (`Q < 0.40`),
  - critical-service boots may remain available for survival recovery.

Behavior
- Boot enqueues a service boot job and completes after subsystem boot time.
- If the service is already running, no new job is queued.

Common block reasons
- `service_missing`
- `deps_unmet`
- `critical_power_state` / `power_quality_critical`
- `brownout_active`

Related commands
- `diag <system_id>`, `power status`, `shutdown <service_name>`, `jobs`
