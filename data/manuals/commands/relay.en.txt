relay uplink

Command status
- Compatibility alias.
- Canonical command: `uplink`.

Equivalent form
- `relay uplink` -> `uplink`

Operational behavior
- Performs uplink intel ingestion on eligible docked relay/station/waystation nodes.
- Applies the same data-core and power gating as `uplink`.

Related commands
