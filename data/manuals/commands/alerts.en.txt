alerts
alerts explain <alert_key>

Purpose
- Inspect current alert surface and read alert-specific diagnosis.

Operational behavior
- `alerts` lists active alerts with current severity.
- `alerts explain <alert_key>` expands conditions, current blockers, and remediation hints.

Usage notes
- Prioritize CRITICAL alerts first.
- For bay maintenance alerts, use `alerts explain drone_bay_maintenance_blocked`.

Related commands
- `status`, `power status`, `logs`
