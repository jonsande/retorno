SYSTEM SUMMARY — life_support
Ref: OS-MAN/SYS/LIFE

`life_support` preserves capsule viability: atmosphere, thermal envelope, and metabolic support.

Function
- Maintains breathable/processable atmosphere and CO2 scrubbing.
- Regulates thermal control envelope for capsule stability.

Operational dependencies
- Treated as critical system at all times.
- If `life_support` reaches OFFLINE, viability grace countdown begins.

Command impact by state
- LIMITED: hibernation blocked.
- CRITICAL: hibernation blocked; survival margin narrowing.
- OFFLINE: viability countdown active; terminal-state lockout risk when grace expires.

Common symptoms
- escalating warnings, survivability pressure, blocked long-duration operations.

Recovery pattern
- restore power delivery,
- prioritize `life_support` repair,
- delay non-essential work until viability stabilizes.

Related commands
- `diag life_support`, `power status`, `drone repair <drone_id> life_support`, `hibernate`
