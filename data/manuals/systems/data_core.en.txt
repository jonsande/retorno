data_core
- Data services and audit subsystem.
- Required for cargo/manifest audit (datad).
- If offline or degraded, inventory audit is blocked.
- Keep at least LIMITED for reliable audits.
- Related commands: boot datad, cargo audit.

Command impact by state
- LIMITED or better: allows cargo audit and auth recover (with datad running).
- BELOW LIMITED (DAMAGED/CRITICAL/OFFLINE): blocks cargo audit and auth recover; uplink blocked if data_core is degraded/offline.
