SYSTEM BRIEF — sensors
Ref: OS-MAN/SYS/SENS

`sensors` is the local signal acquisition and detection layer.

Role
- Detect nearby echoes/contacts through `scan`.
- Feeds `route solve` within range.
- Provides telemetry input for `ship survey` together with `data_core`.

Boot requirements
- Requires `energy_distribution >= nominal`.
- Service: `sensord` (boot separately).

Command impact by state
- `scan`: requires `sensors >= limited` and `sensord` running.
- `route solve`: requires `sensors >= limited` and `sensord` running (plus power/range conditions).
- `ship survey`: requires `sensors >= limited` and `sensord` running (plus `data_core >= limited` + `datad`).
- OFFLINE or stopped `sensord`: sensor telemetry unavailable.

Relevant commands
- `boot sensord`, `diag sensors`, `scan`, `route solve`, `ship survey`.
