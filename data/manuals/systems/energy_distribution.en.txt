SYSTEM SUMMARY — energy_distribution
Ref: OS-MAN/SYS/DIST

`energy_distribution` is the ship grid routing layer: bus conditioning, phase control, and load delivery.

Function
- Delivers generated power from `power_core` to subsystem loads.
- Maintains bus quality and suppresses instability propagation.

Operational dependencies
- Multiple subsystem boots require distribution at nominal-grade state.
- Distribution OFFLINE triggers protective shutdown behavior for non-critical systems.

Command impact by state
- BELOW NOMINAL: dependency-gated subsystem boots may be blocked.
- LIMITED/DAMAGED/CRITICAL: higher quality penalties and instability pressure.
- OFFLINE: forces non-critical systems OFFLINE and disables bay maintenance support.

Common symptoms
- `low_power_quality`, `power_bus_instability`, cascading service interruptions.

Recovery pattern
- prioritize `repair energy_distribution`,
- shed non-critical load,
- stabilize `Q` before resuming complex operations.

Related commands
- `diag energy_distribution`, `power status`, `drone repair <drone_id> energy_distribution`, `alerts`
