SYSTEM SUMMARY — drone_bay
Ref: OS-MAN/SYS/DRN-BAY

drone_bay is the ship subsystem for drone handling, bay-assisted maintenance, and module bay operations.

Function
- Handles bay-assisted drone workflows (`deploy`, `recall`, `reboot`, module operations).
- Recharges docked drones.
- Decontaminates docked drones.
- Applies passive integrity repair to docked drones when support conditions are met.

Operational dependencies
- Bay maintenance support requires:
  - `drone_bay` not `OFFLINE`,
  - `energy_distribution` not `OFFLINE`.
- Charging also depends on instantaneous power conditions (net generation / reserve).
- Drone-module bay operations (`drone install` with `scope=drone`, `drone uninstall`) require:
  - target drone docked in `DRN-BAY`,
  - `drone_bay == NOMINAL`,
  - `energy_distribution != OFFLINE`.
- Ship-module install/uninstall (legacy operator flow) require bay support online (`drone_bay` not `OFFLINE`) but do not require `NOMINAL`.

Command impact by state
- NOMINAL:
  - Baseline ETA for bay-assisted jobs.
  - Drone-module bay operations allowed.
  - Normal charge/decon/passive repair support.
- LIMITED:
  - Bay-assisted ETAs are slower.
  - Drone-module bay operations blocked (`drone_bay_not_nominal`).
  - Ship-module install/uninstall remain available with slowdown.
  - Reduced maintenance throughput.
- DAMAGED:
  - Bay-assisted ETAs significantly slower.
  - Integrity-hit risk during bay-assisted movement phases (`deploy`/`recall`).
  - Drone-module bay operations blocked (`drone_bay_not_nominal`).
  - Ship-module install/uninstall remain available with slowdown.
- CRITICAL:
  - Severe slowdown plus degraded passive-repair reliability.
  - Drone-module bay operations blocked (`drone_bay_not_nominal`).
  - Ship-module install/uninstall remain available with severe slowdown.
- OFFLINE:
  - Bay support unavailable.
  - Drone-module bay operations blocked (`drone_bay_install_offline`).

Common symptoms
- Docked drones stop charging while needing battery.
- Docked drones stop recovering integrity while needing repair.
- `drone_bay_maintenance_blocked` alert remains active.
- Drone-module install/uninstall blocked despite module availability.

Recovery pattern
- Restore `drone_bay` and `energy_distribution` above `OFFLINE` for maintenance support.
- For drone-module operations, restore `drone_bay` specifically to `NOMINAL`.
- Validate power net and scrap availability when passive repair is expected.

Related commands
- `diag drone_bay`, `drone status [drone_id]`, `drone install <drone_id> <module_id>`, `drone uninstall <drone_id> <module_id>`, `alerts explain drone_bay_maintenance_blocked`
