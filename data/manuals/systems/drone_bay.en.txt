SYSTEM SUMMARY — drone_bay
Ref: OS-MAN/SYS/DRN-BAY

drone_bay is the ship subsystem for drone handling, support, and lifecycle operations.

Function
- Handles deploy/recall/reboot/install workflows from the bay.
- Recharges docked drone batteries.
- Decontaminates docked drone radiation dose.
- Applies passive integrity repair to docked drones (scrap cost applies).

Operational dependencies
- For bay support (charging, decontamination, passive repair):
  - `drone_bay` must not be `OFFLINE`.
  - `energy_distribution` must not be `OFFLINE`.
- Battery charging also depends on instant power availability:
  - enough net power (`P_gen - P_load`) for normal charging, or
  - ship battery reserve (`SoC > 0`) for reduced charging.
- drone_bay no longer requires nominal energy_distribution dependency to be powered on.

Command impact by state
- NOMINAL:
  - Baseline ETA for `drone deploy`, `drone recall`, `drone install`, `drone reboot`.
  - Normal charging/decontamination/passive repair behavior.
- LIMITED:
  - Slower `deploy/recall/install/reboot`.
  - Slower charging.
  - Slower passive repair.
- DAMAGED:
  - Much slower `deploy/recall/install/reboot`.
  - Much slower charging.
  - Minor integrity loss risk during `deploy`/`recall`.
  - Passive repair remains slow and costs more scrap.
- CRITICAL:
  - Same severe slowdown profile as degraded bay states.
  - Minor integrity risk on `deploy`/`recall`.
  - Passive repair is even slower, more expensive, and may fail with minor drone damage.
- OFFLINE:
  - Normal `drone deploy` is blocked.
  - `drone install` is blocked.
  - `drone recall` remains allowed (without maintenance support).
  - No charging, no decontamination, no passive repair.

EMERGENCY ADVICE
- If you must launch while bay support is down, use `drone deploy!`.
- `deploy!` enables emergency override (including critical state and bay OFFLINE), but it does NOT bypass physical prerequisites:
  - drone must exist and be `docked` (not `disabled`, not already deployed),
  - minimum deploy integrity must be met,
  - target must be valid (`ship_sector` or a `world_node` where ship is docked).
- `deploy!` also does not bypass global terminal lockouts (`terminal_state`).
- `deploy!` enqueues a risk job (glitch/failure). It can end with integrity loss or deploy failure.
- If `energy_distribution` is `OFFLINE`, `deploy!` runs under emergency maintenance bypass and remains high-risk.

Related alerts
- `drone_bay_charging_unavailable`
- `drone_bay_maintenance_blocked` (see `alerts explain drone_bay_maintenance_blocked`)

Related commands
- `drone deploy`, `drone deploy!`, `drone recall`, `drone reboot`, `drone install`
- `diag drone_bay`, `alerts`, `alerts explain drone_bay_maintenance_blocked`
