ALERT — low_power_quality

Meaning
- Power-quality factor `Q` is below stable nominal range.

Typical causes
- low SoC, sustained net deficit, damaged `energy_distribution`.

Escalation impact
- `Q < 0.55`: complex operations blocked (`uplink`, `route solve`, `auth recover`).
- `Q < 0.40`: hibernate and non-critical boot blocked.
- `Q < 0.25`: non-critical shedding/collapse pressure increases.

Immediate response
- stabilize distribution,
- reduce non-critical load,
- rebuild SoC reserve.
