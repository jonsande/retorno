drone_bay_maintenance_blocked
- At least one docked drone in `drone_bay` needs battery/integrity recovery, but bay support conditions are not met.
- The alert stays active while any affected docked drone remains blocked.

Battery charging requires:
- drone status `docked` in `drone_bay`
- `drone_bay` not `OFFLINE`
- `energy_distribution` not `OFFLINE`
- power gate:
  - normal charge if `net_kw >= DRONE_CHARGE_KW`
  - slow charge if `SoC > 0` and `net_kw >= DRONE_CHARGE_NET_MIN_KW`

Passive integrity repair requires:
- drone status `docked` in `drone_bay` and integrity < 1.0
- `drone_bay` and `energy_distribution` available (not `OFFLINE`)
- enough cargo scrap for current passive-repair cost

Drone decontamination requires:
- drone status `docked` in `drone_bay`
- `drone_bay` not `OFFLINE`
- `energy_distribution` not `OFFLINE`
- no net-power threshold and no scrap cost
