ALERT ‚Äî drone_bay_maintenance_blocked

Meaning
- One or more drones docked in `drone_bay` need battery/integrity recovery, but bay support conditions are not satisfied.
- Alert remains active while any affected docked drone stays blocked.

Battery charging conditions
- drone status `docked` in `drone_bay`
- `drone_bay` not `OFFLINE`
- `energy_distribution` not `OFFLINE`
- power gate:
  - normal charging if `net_kw >= DRONE_CHARGE_KW`
  - reduced charging if `SoC > 0` and `net_kw >= DRONE_CHARGE_NET_MIN_KW`

Passive integrity-repair conditions
- drone status `docked` in `drone_bay` and integrity `< 1.0`
- `drone_bay` and `energy_distribution` available (not `OFFLINE`)
- enough cargo scrap for current passive-repair cost

Drone decontamination conditions
- drone status `docked` in `drone_bay`
- `drone_bay` not `OFFLINE`
- `energy_distribution` not `OFFLINE`
- no net-power threshold and no scrap cost

Immediate response
- restore bay/distribution availability,
- restore charging headroom,
- ensure scrap stock for passive repair.
