radiation (mvp)
- Radiation is modeled in two layers:
  - env radiation: effective ambient radiation at current node or transit lane.
  - internal radiation: env radiation filtered by hull integrity.

hull coupling
- `hull` is a global ship property (0..1), not a regular ship system.
- Lower hull integrity increases radiation ingress into internal systems.
- Internal radiation accelerates existing system wear through each system's `k_rad`.

systems
- Radiation does not replace degradation logic.
- It only adds pressure to normal wear (power quality + base decay + radiation factor).
- No lore/intel/data corruption is applied in this MVP.

drones
- Non-docked drones accumulate radiation dose over time.
- Higher dose increases drone integrity wear using dose bands.
- Docked drones are automatically decontaminated if drone_bay and energy_distribution support is available.

scope limits
- No random travel impact events in this MVP.
- No terminal hull fail/game-over path in this MVP.
- The hull damage path is prepared for future impact/event integration.
